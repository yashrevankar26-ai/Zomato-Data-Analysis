## Project Overview
This project analyzes Zomato sales and order data to uncover actionable insights into customer behavior, restaurant performance, and revenue trends. Interactive dashboards were created to support data-driven decision-making for business growth.

## Objectives
- Analyze order trends, revenue growth, and customer demand patterns.
- Identify peak ordering hours and high-demand periods.
- Evaluate restaurant and cuisine performance.
- Measure contribution of top-performing entities to total revenue.
- Build interactive dashboards for quick and effective business analysis.

## Tools & Technologies
- **Excel**: Data cleaning, preprocessing, pivot tables, charts
- **SQL**: Data querying, aggregations, joins, KPI and percentage calculations
- **Power BI**: Data modeling, KPI visualization, interactive dashboards
- **Tableau**: Advanced visualizations, trend analysis, and comparative insights

## Key Insights
- Most orders occur during peak hours, representing a significant portion of daily activity.
- A relatively small group of restaurants drives the majority of total revenue.
- Popular cuisines dominate customer preferences and account for over half of total orders.
- Weekend demand is noticeably higher compared to weekdays, showing clear trends in ordering behavior.
- High-demand locations contribute substantially to overall sales, highlighting key areas for business focus.

## Outcomes
- Interactive Power BI and Tableau dashboards enabled faster business insights.
- Improved understanding of customer demand, ordering behavior, and revenue distribution.
- Insights helped identify growth opportunities through popular cuisines, peak hours, and top restaurants.
- Data-driven recommendations to optimize pricing, promotions, and restaurant partnerships.
